# Question 83

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: What duty does the second commandment teach us?**

*Answer: To worship God correctly, and to avoid idolatry.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: What does the second commandment teach us?**

*Answer: To worship God in a proper manner, and to avoid idolatry.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
Supplies the word 'duty' to continue the sectional theme. Additionally the phrase 'proper manner' is simplified to 'correctly'.
