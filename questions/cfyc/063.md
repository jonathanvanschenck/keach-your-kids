# Question 63

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: What did these sacrifices represent?**

*Answer: Christ as the Lamb of God, who would die for sinners.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: What did these sacrifices represent?**

*Answer: Christ, the Lamb of God, who was to die for sinners.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
We include the "as" to highlight the representation pointing at Christ's role specifically.
Additinally, "was to" is a little awkward, so we change it to simply "would".
