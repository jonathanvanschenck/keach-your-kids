# Question 57

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: What does it mean to believe in Christ?**

*Answer: It is faith: trusting that Christ alone saves us.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: What is it to believe or have faith in Christ?**

*Answer: To trust in Christ alone for salvation.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
"What is it to" is changed to "What does it mean to" for consistency with [question 56](/cfyc/questions/56).
Additionally, rather than embedding the synonym of "believe" and "have faith" in the questoin, we
make it explicit in the answer. This also makes the question a cleaner clarification of the answer
to [question 55](/cfyc/questions/55).
