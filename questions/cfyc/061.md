# Question 61

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: How were God's people saved before Christ came?**

*Answer: By believing in the Savior who was to come.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: How were pious persons saved before the coming of Christ?**

*Answer: By believing in a Savior to come.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
"Pious persons" is changed to "God's people", since it is the piety of the person
is actually immaterial to their salvation. Rather, it is their status as part of God's elect people
that matters. Granted, this different could be due to a historical connotation of "pious persons"
that is lost to modern audiences.
