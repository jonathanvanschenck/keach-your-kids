# Question 78

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: Is God pleased with those who love and obey him?**

*Answer: Yes; he says in Proverbs 8:17, “I love those who love me, and those who seek me diligently find me.”*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: Is God pleased with those who love and obey him?**

*Answer: Yes; he says, “I love them that love me.”*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
Adds the scriptural reference, and updates from KJV to ESV, also expands the reference to the entire verse
