# Question 79

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: Is God displeased with those who do not love and obey him?**

*Answer: Yes; he says in Psalm 7:11, “God is a righteous judge, and a God who feels indignation every day.”*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: Is God displeased with those who do not love and obey him?**

*Answer: Yes; “God is angry with the wicked every day.”*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
Adds the scriptural reference, and updates from KJV to ESV, also expands the reference to the entire verse
