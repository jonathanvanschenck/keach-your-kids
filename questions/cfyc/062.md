# Question 62

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: How did they show their faith?**

*Answer: By offering proper sacrifices on God’s altar.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: How did they show their faith?**

*Answer: By offering sacrifices on God’s altar.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
We add "proper" to clarify that not just any sacrifice would do. The sacrifices
had to be in accordance with God's law, which is alluded to in the original
answer by the reference to "God's altar" -- the only acceptable location for
sacrifices.
