# Question 59

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: How can you get the power and help of the Holy Spirit?**

*Answer: God has told us that we must pray to Him and ask for the Holy Spirit.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: How can you get the help of the Holy Spirit?**

*Answer: God has told us that we must pray to him for the Holy Spirit.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
For the last several questions, "help" has been modified to "power", but
at this point, we retain "help" as a the relevent work, but add "power" for
continuity. Additionally, we add "and ask" to the answer to make it more explicit.
