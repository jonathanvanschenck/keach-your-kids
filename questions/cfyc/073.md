# Question 73

---
## Modern Adaptation
**Question: What is duty?**

*Answer: Duty is what God requires us to think, say and do.*

---
## Traditional Formulation
**Question: What are the ten commandments sometimes called?**

*Answer: The Decalogue.*

---
## Adaptation Commentary
The ordering here is modified; we start with the explaining duty before describing that
explaining the commandments as duties. This seems more important than ensuring a child
knowns a particular greek work.
